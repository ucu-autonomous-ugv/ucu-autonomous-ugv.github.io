Welcome to the **UCU Autonomous UGV Lab & Student Club**!

We are thrilled to open our virtual doors to students, researchers, and robotics enthusiasts. Our mission is to develop next-generation autonomous ground vehicles (UGVs) capable of operating in rugged, unstructured, and challenging environments.

### What is the UGV Club?
The UGV Club is an initiative at the Ukrainian Catholic University focused on hands-on robotics development. We combine academic research with practical implementation:
- **Autonomous Navigation**: Deploying state-of-the-art SLAM and trajectory planning.
- **Hardware Integration**: Customizing physical rover platforms with LiDAR, stereo cameras, and powerful edge compute modules.
- **Open Collaboration**: Sharing our findings and software with the wider developer community.

### How to Join
We are always looking for passionate people to join us:
1. **Software Developers**: Familiar with ROS 2, C++, Python, or Web interfaces.
2. **Hardware Builders**: Interested in electronic wiring, mechanical assembly, or CAD modeling.
3. **Writers & Creatives**: To help document our progress and share it with the world.

Feel free to visit us in the lab or drop a message via our [Reach Us](/reach/) page!
