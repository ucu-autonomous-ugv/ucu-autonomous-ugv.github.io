About the club

Who are we

In light of the full-scale Russian invasion of Ukraine, which has demonstrated the strategic and humanitarian importance of autonomous robotics, our group is dedicated to advancing the state-of-the-art in UGV algorithms.

Our work focuses on both software and hardware development, with a particular emphasis on the development of autonomous navigation and control algorithms.
